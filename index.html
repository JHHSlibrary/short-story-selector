Welcome to the D214 Short Story Selector -- your place for on-demand reading.  A constantly growing collection, the Short Story Collector gives you random access to short stories of many genres and authors, recent and classic. Here you will find modern authors like Stephen King, Elizabeth Acevedo and Melissa Maar, plus classic authors like Virginia Woolf, Edgar Allan Poe, Kurt Vonnegut and more.

Stories are identified by genre and also length.

As the stories are not identified by number of pages, we have attempted to gauge the length in terms of "reading time."

Short: less than 15 or 20 minutes to read. Most poetry can be read in less than 5 minutes.
Medium: approximately 20-45 minutes to read.
Long: These stories may take 45 minutes or longer to read.

This is just a guide, as everybody reads at different rates.

All of these stories can be found online, and are embedded to allow for proper copyright acknowledgement. Some stories are older and in the public domain.

If there is an issue with a story that appears in this Short Story Selector, please contact us at jhhs.library@d214.org.
